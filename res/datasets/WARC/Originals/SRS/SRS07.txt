SRS 7 - The universal header "warc.h" shall provide normalised interfaces to enable developers to create valid and compliant WARC-records, based on the definition in the "ISO TC 46/SC 4 N 595" standards document. The interfaces shall be made available to create WARC records of the following types:
- "warinfo"
- "response"
- "request"
- "metadata"
- "revisit"
- "conversion"
- "continuation"
- "resource"
